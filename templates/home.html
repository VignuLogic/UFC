How We Operate
🐄
Step 01
Gaushala Affiliated by UFC
Pure A2 milk sourced from healthy Desi cows raised in our affiliated Gaushalas across Gujarat villages.
🚛
Step 02
Collection Centre
Milk collected daily from trusted village farms and transported fresh to our processing facility.
🏭
Step 03
UFC Factory
Our factory in Mathak, Halvad, Morbi churns A2 milk into premium ice creams in small batches daily.
🏪
Step 04
UFC Outlets
Fresh ice cream reaches 35+ outlets across Ahmedabad, Baroda, Mehsana, Indore and growing.
😊
Step 05
Happy Consumers
You enjoy pure, natural ice cream — and every scoop supports a Gujarat farmer's livelihood.
